SHIMLA

Shimla is the capital and the largest city of the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. In 1864, Shimla was declared as the summer capital of British India.

Dharamshala is a major hill station and spiritual center. ... It hosts a number of trekking trails across the Himalayas into the upper Ravi Valley and Chamba .

CHAMBA

Chamba the land of lord Shiva is famous for its untouched natural beauty. The district has Dalhousie, Khajjiar, Chamba Town, Pangi and Bharamour as main tourist destinations.

MANALI

Manali is a popular tourist destination in India and serves as the gateway to the Lahaul and Spiti district as well as the city of Leh in Ladakh.
